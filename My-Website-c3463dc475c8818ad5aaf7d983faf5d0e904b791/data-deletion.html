Data Deletion Instructions

Ke Aupuni O Ke Akua Press - Social Media Manager Application

What Data We Collect

Our Social Media Manager application uses Facebook Login to authenticate users and post content on their behalf. We collect and store:

  • Your Facebook User ID
  • Your name and email address
  • Access tokens for posting to your Facebook pages
  • Page IDs of pages you manage

How to Request Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of all your data from our application. To do so:

Method 1: Revoke App Access (Immediate)

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings
  2. Click on "Apps and Websites"
  3. Find "Social Media Manager" or "AutoPoster"
  4. Click "Remove"

This immediately revokes our application's access to your Facebook data.

Method 2: Email Request

Send a data deletion request to:

Email: hoaaina61@gmail.com

Subject Line: "Data Deletion Request - Social Media Manager"

Include in your email:

  • Your Facebook name
  • Your Facebook User ID (if known)
  • Your email address associated with the app

What Happens After Deletion Request

Once you revoke app access or send a deletion request:

  • Within 24 hours: Your access tokens will be invalidated
  • Within 7 days: All personal data will be permanently deleted from our systems
  • Within 30 days: All backup data will be purged

Data We Retain

After deletion, we may retain anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes only. This data cannot be traced back to you personally.
